I'm Dungnm - Web designer at Zozo.
I am responsible for the appearance of the products before sending them to the customer. Besides the interface, I always try to improve the customer's experience when using the products in the best possible way. My goal is to get better at Ui/Ux.

"arrow"

A quote from Le Corbusier that says:

"I prefer drawing to talking.
Drawing is faster and leaves less room for lies"

There are usually some notebook and a black marker on my desk so that I can write down every idea that comes to my mind. This is also a part of the process when I fulfill a client's design requirements.

"lotus"

One of the design concepts that I always pursue is "Less is more". By minimizing unnecessary details and focusing on the most important product values, that will help me increase the customer conversion rates.

"moonlight"

Although I am a designer, my biggest weakness lies in creating illustrations. I find myself more confident in solving problems relating to the layout presentation, the order, synchronization and consistency in designs to satisfy the customers' requirements than when I have to illustrate something.

"bad blood"

"It is always the best experience to watch our clients happy and satisfied"

Creating a product that makes yourself happy is incomparable with creating a product that satisfies the customers and meets their demanding requirements. Well, of course, if the product makes both you and the customers happy, that's even better!
